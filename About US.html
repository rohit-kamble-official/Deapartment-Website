About the Department

Electrical Engineering deals with the study and application of electricity, electronics and electromagnetism. Being a core field of engineering, it provides better career opportunities and sustainable job prospects.

Our graduates work with top organizations like NTPC, BHEL, POWERGRID, State Distribution Companies, L&T, ABB, Siemens, Coal India, NHPC, and several international firms. The renewable energy boom—including hydel, solar, and wind—demands quality professionals in Electrical Engineering.

Final-year students tackle practical and industrial problems in Power Systems, Machines, Power Electronics, Drives, and Control. Regular workshops and industrial visits enhance real-world exposure.

Skill development, personality grooming, and entrepreneurship are encouraged. The department boasts fully-equipped labs and highly qualified faculty with rich industry experience.

Welcome from the Head of Department

It is my pleasure to welcome you to the Department of Electrical Engineering. With the growing demand for Electrical Engineers in government and private sectors, the department is committed to producing highly trained and capable engineers who can take on real-world challenges. We have a fine blend of experienced and dynamic faculty members dedicated to delivering the highest quality education.

Our lab facilities are continuously upgraded to encourage innovation and hands-on learning. The department boasts fully equipped laboratories, including High Voltage Lab, Power Electronics Lab, Electrical Machines Lab, Linear Electric Circuit Lab, Control Systems Lab, Electrical Measurement & Instrumentation Lab, Electrical Workshop, and more.

Internship programs and industrial visits are regularly organized to enhance practical knowledge and expose students to current technologies. Our student-led departmental forum, ELAN, hosts technical and cultural events to nurture creativity and leadership.

For any suggestions or queries, feel free to contact us at hodelectrical@sbjit.edu.in.
Be a part of this great journey of creativity and knowledge.
