Forum Description

Electrical League of Ampere’s Nexus

The inception of Students Forum came from the concept that students should be able to generate and organize ideas for their growth. The Forum is entirely managed by students under staff guidance and organizes all technical and extra-curricular activities.

Objectives:

  1. Provide a platform for student development.
  2. Ensure smooth activity execution under guidance.
  3. Connect with alumni and industry.
  4. Enhance student interactions.
  5. Encourage research perspective and innovation.
  6. Develop leadership and management skills.
  7. Support training and placements.
  8. Promote expression through writing and creativity.
  9. Foster freedom of expression.
  10. Provide a space for idea exchange.
