About IEEE PES

The IEEE Power & Energy Society (PES) is a worldwide, non-profit association of more than 40,000 industry professionals focused on the scientific and engineering knowledge about electric power and energy. Our chapter aims to bridge academia and industry through workshops, seminars, and collaborative events.
